What is CATI?

Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviewing (CATI) is a sophisticated survey methodology where trained interviewers conduct telephone surveys using specialized software that guides the interview process, records responses in real-time, and ensures data quality through built-in validation.

CATI represents the perfect blend of traditional telephone research and cutting-edge technology, enabling efficient, accurate, and real-time data collection through expertly conducted phone surveys.
