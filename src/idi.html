In-Depth Interviews (IDI)

Uncovering Deep Insights Through Qualitative Excellence

What are In-Depth Interviews?

IDIs are qualitative research interviews conducted individually with carefully selected respondents. These semi-structured conversations typically last 30-90 minutes and explore topics in comprehensive detail.

They allow for deep exploration of motivations, experiences, and perspectives impossible to capture through quantitative methods alone.
